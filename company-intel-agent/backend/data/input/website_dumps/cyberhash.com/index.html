It’s tough leading security.

Competing priorities and information overload.

Having to be close to the ground, but maintain the distance to see the bigger picture.

Needing the right people, with the right skills, at the right time.

Cyberhash reduces the noise and connects the dots between
your pain points and the best solutions for you.

Helping you achieve your security goals while you remain the face of security.

Cyberhash was founded on three pillars

  • Do Good

    We’re open and direct.

    There’s no point in us recommending solutions if they’re not relevant to your business.

    We’re your trusted advisor and we take that trust seriously.

  • Be Good

    We care about you.

    Cyber Security only works effectively if its embedded with people in mind.

    We work closely with you to bake in sustainable change for your business, making it more adaptable, safe, and secure.

  • Build Good

    We get it. We’ve been there.

    Building security teams can be challenging. Balancing business priorities with security can be tough.

    We’re a team of experts who understand what it’s really like, and work alongside you - the expert in your business.

Meet Manoj, Founder of Cyberhash

“I started Cyberhash after feeling frustrated with the way that cyber security services were being delivered to organisations. Cyberhash was founded with a fresh approach to Cyber Security, ensuring that services are delivered together.

I felt that it was missing the people-element - the connection between the client and the consultant. I wanted to create a business that does good, enables clients to grow and thrive, and is a great place to work.

With Cyberhash I want to create a long-term impact that works better for everyone.”
