Capture. Assure. Access.

A complete framework for regulated data. From seamless capture to assured compliance and secure, actionable access.

Capture: Capture data from every source

CC1 captures regulated digital data from live, historic, cloud, and on-prem sources, with a rapidly growing list of compatible channels.

The toolkit for compliant communications

Capture regulated data from any source with industry-leading Five-Nines (99.999%) capture availability. Five-Nines is the gold standard for system uptime, keeping theoretical downtime to an absolute minimum, mitigating the risks of non-compliance.

 

From trader turrets to recording Microsoft Teams, Cisco Webex, and Zoom, we ensure your organization can leverage a full suite of compliant communication channels. We can also capture IoT and enterprise data, such as HR and CRM.

Assure: Streamline global compliance

CC1 delivers a unified regulated data service across global organizations, consolidating data into a secure, simple-to-view, and audit-ready archive.

Reconcile media & metadata in near real time

CC1 is designed to mitigate breaches of regulations such as MiFID II, MAR, and SEC 17-a3/17a-4 that can incur significant financial and reputational penalties.

Near-real-time reconciliation, automated alerting, governance checks, audit processes, built-in encryption, and write-once-read-many (WORM) storage give you the tools you need to comply.

Access: Unlock next-gen decision-making

CC1 ensures a reliable and secure data source to power advanced surveillance, analysis, artificial intelligence, and machine learning platforms.

Transform data into actionable insights

Unlock the latest data analysis, artificial intelligence, and machine learning platforms to drive decision-making and surveillance across your global organization.
