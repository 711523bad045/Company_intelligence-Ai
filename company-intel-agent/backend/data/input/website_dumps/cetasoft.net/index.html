Key features

Bidding

Win work faster with customisable templates that ensure accuracy and consistency. Track actuals, apply discounts or markups, and maintain control over margins.

Scheduling

Manage people, rooms, equipment, and licences in one place. Drag and drop scheduling across time zones, sync calendars in real time, and log accurate time and costs .
