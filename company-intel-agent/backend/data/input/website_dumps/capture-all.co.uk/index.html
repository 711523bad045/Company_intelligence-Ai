Services We Offer

Our range of digitisation services includes document scanning, specialist book scanning, large-scale drawing scanning and scan-on-demand. Where demand is urgent, our fast-track one-hour request service can help you quickly deal with those unexpected emergencies.

We also offer arranged pick-up services, with our drivers taking your documents to our secure storage facilities via safe-fitted and GPS-tracked vehicles. Alternatively, if you would like your documents to be destroyed we can arrange for them to be shredded and recycled.

Scanning Services in Glasgow, Edinburgh and More

Businesses across Scotland have access to timely, solution-based document scanning and storage wherever theyre based. Our services extend from Aberdeen and Inverness in the north, down the east coast through Dundee, Perth, Kirkcaldy, Dunfermline and over the Forth Road Bridge to Edinburgh.

How Our Document Scanning and Storage Services Work

Collection and Preparation

Our driver will collect your documents on a pre-arranged date and time. They will then be carefully sorted, de-stapled and prepared for scanning.

Scanning and Indexing:

Using the appropriate scanner, your documents are digitised to your unique specifications. Files are renamed and metadata is applied to facilitate future retrieval.

Quality Checks and Digital Storage:

Multiple quality checks are carried out during and after the scanning process. Scanned files are then backed up and written to secure digital media for delivery.

Document Storage:

If required, we can provide secure storage for a cost-effective and flexible approach to archiving your documents.

Delivery:

Your documents will be returned to you either physically or electronically – whichever method you choose you can rely on us for security and expediency.

Shred or Return:

If you elect to have your documents destroyed, we can securely shred and recycle them to safeguard any personal or sensitive information.
