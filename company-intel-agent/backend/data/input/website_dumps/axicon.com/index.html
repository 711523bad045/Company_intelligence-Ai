BARCODE VERIFIERS

All Axicon barcode verifiers conform to the latest ISO/IEC print quality and verifier standards. Our equipment has been independently tested, and conforms to ISO/IEC 15426-1 and 15426-2. They all have USB connections and are provided with free software.

Barcode images

As well as barcode verifiers, Axicon has been producing high-quality barcode images for retail and commercial use since 1979, and our years of experience mean that we can provide you with the correct barcode for your particular print job.

Check digit calculator

The check digit is calculated from all the preceding digits, and it is used by any scanning system to check that the number scanned in from a barcode is correct.
