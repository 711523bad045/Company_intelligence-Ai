The era of information technology raises the need of 24/7 surveillance of the omnipresent cyber threats. Services

Offensive Security

Offensive Security is an in-depth examination of the information technology processes and structures a business has established. This assessment looks to identify possible security risks, and how they can be mitigated.

Governance Risk and Compliance

We provide is a structured way to align IT with business goals while managing risks and meeting all industry and government regulations. It includes tools and processes to unify an organization's governance and risk management with its technological innovation and adoption.

Management Systems Certifications

We aim to provide you a framework for the establishment, maintenance and continuous improvement of internal quality processes.

Education and Training

We are offering a wide range of internationally accredited IT security training in addition to boot camps for certification exams globally.

Managed Security Service

Our Managed SOC Service provides customers with a cost-effective, advanced intrusion detection capability which enables them to identify and respond to cyber attacks.

Tools and Solutions

We not only identify your security problems but also provide solutions. Our range consists of products from perimeter networks to end-point security.
