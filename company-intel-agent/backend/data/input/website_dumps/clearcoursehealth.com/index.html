Why ClearCourse Health?

ClearCourse Health is the technology partner your healthcare business needs, with a suite of purpose-built solutions that have evolved over two decades in collaboration with healthcare providers.


The ClearCourse Health platform helps you manage your patient journey and your healthcare organisation, helping deliver exceptional patient experiences, reducing time spent on admin and creating more time to care. 


After all, healthcare businesses are busy places, and they rely on fast, accurate information that staff and patients can trust. 


From making appointments to invoicing and taking payment, keeping clinical records to charting and sharing patient progress, having a system you can rely on is essential. 


Often, healthcare businesses have multiple software systems taking care of various tasks. However, multiple software solutions bring a number of headaches, including multiple subscription fees and integration challenges – as well as more security risks. 


Bringing together established best-in class software solutions from Blue Zinc, TM3, Caseflow, e-clinic, Pronto, Click and PPM – ClearCourse Health provides everything you need in one platform.


You can choose the features you need, and leave the features you don’t – safe in the knowledge they are there when you need them.
