A DATANET CASE STUDY – MEDIA SOLUTIONS
ORGANISATION:

Dalet

Due to the demanding nature of the media solutions that Dalet provide, they were looking for a high power rack (6kW ~24Amps) with a generous allocation of Internet bandwidth (dual 10Gb) in a high security facility with 247 access for their engineers. Preferably a half hour from their Basingstoke offices.

Dalet would also appreciate help with the actual move of their servers for which Datanet were able to offer use of our van, ramp and flight cases.
