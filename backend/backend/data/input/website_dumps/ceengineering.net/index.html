HPE NonStop, HP/Compaq ServerNet and Tandem Computers System Specialists

CE Engineering provides hardware and software support, hardware sales, and system management for legacy HPE NonStop Systems, HP/Compaq ServerNet S-Series Systems and Tandem Computers Himalaya K-Series Systems. This family of computers have historically been referred to as 'Tandem Systems' though the HPE trademark 'NonStop' is now more widely used.

The Tandem/NonStop family are high availability, fault-tolerant computer systems which are typically deployed to run mission-critical applications in banking, finance, retail, telecommunications, manufacturing, healthcare and defence.

As Tandem/HPE NonStop specialists, CE Engineering does not sell or support generic HPE product such as Proliant Windows/LINUX servers, Integrity Unix servers, nor HP desktop PCs, laptops, printers or any other hardware for the domestic market. If you have arrived at this web site looking for help with product that is not 'NonStop', unfortunately we will not be able to help you.

Global Cover

Since its foundation in 1989, CE Engineering has supplied NonStop equipment and support services to customers in Africa, Asia, Australasia, Canada, Europe, Indonesia, the Middle East, Scandinavia, South America, the United Kingdom (including all the major banks and financial institutions in the UK), and the USA. We have also supplied equipment, support services and consultancy to a number of HPE offices around the world.

HPE NonStop, ServerNet and Tandem EoSL Sales and Support

Within the NonStop Tandem market CE Engineering focuses on the sales and support of legacy systems. Legacy Tandem/HPE NonStop systems are those which are no longer sold by the manufacturer and which the manufacturer has deemed to be close to End of Service Life (EoSL - also known as End of Life or EoL) or on which the manufacturer has withdrawn hardware support completely.

Our mission is to extend the operating life of Tandem/NonStop systems for the full useable life of those systems and for as long as there is a need for the systems.

Hardware Sales and Inventory

CE Engineering sells refurbished and purchases used HPE NonStop (Tandem) Systems, components and peripherals. NonStop servers and components may be sold or leased, and customers' redundant systems purchased outright or re-marketed on a commission basis.

We hold a large inventory of used and refurbished Tandem and HPE NonStop hardware at our facilities in the United Kingdom and at our sister company in the USA. And, through close links with HPE NonStop (Tandem) users and specialist brokers around the world we have access to volumes of additional equipment. NonStop Hardware Maintenance 24 x 365

Hardware maintenance is at the core of CE Engineering's services. Capitalising on the relative ease of maintenance of Tandem and HPE NonStop systems, CE Engineering has developed a hardware maintenance package that offers cost savings over normal support contracts by reducing the need for a team of travelling customer engineers.

CE Engineering's hardware support package has additional benefits in that problem response and rectification times are reduced as the delay in waiting for a trained engineer to arrive on-site is all but eliminated. Furthermore, logistical delay can be eradicated through the provision of an on-site hot-spare system.

We specialise in providing support for 'legacy' equipment that HPE has deemed to be beyond 'End of Service Life' (EoSL), contracting to continue support for the production life of the system(s). Read more…
