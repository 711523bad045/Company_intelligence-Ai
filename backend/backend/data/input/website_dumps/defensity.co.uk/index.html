How can we solve your cyber security challenges?

Protecting your business from cyber security threats can be challenging. This is especially true if you are a new startup or SME on a tight budget. Thankfully, our team at Defensity can help you thwart attacks before they happen.

Data is the currency of the 21st century. However, this means that the more data you process, the more likely you are to be targeted by cyber criminals. Defensity, therefore, offers a range of cyber security services that can be customised to almost all industry sectors and SME budgets.

Read More

From security audits to 24/7 virtual CISO support, we work first and foremost to help you minimize the risk of cyber-attacks. We do this by implementing lightweight, yet robust cyber security frameworks, that offer total protection from common security threats. Most importantly of all, we never over complicate things.


At Defensity, we can help you gain NCSC Cyber Essentials certification for your business. This will help you become more security aware and immediately build better trust among consumers and industry partners. We can also monitor your network security remotely, as well as be on-hand instantly if you ever suspect that an attack is in progress.


At present, 43% of all cyber-attacks in the UK are directed toward SMEs. With the average cost of recovery after attacks standing at 65,000, you cant afford to take cyber security for granted. Now with Defensity, you dont have to.
