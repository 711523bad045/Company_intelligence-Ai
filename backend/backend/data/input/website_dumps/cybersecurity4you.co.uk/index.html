ABOUT US

Cyber Security 4 You is a trading name for Cyber21 Limited. The company was established in 2022 and has focused on providing pragmatic, affordable and cost-effective services for small and medium sized businesses.

Based in the United Kingdom and Cyprus, the company provides both cyber security services and managed solutions. Our goal is to minimise the risk exposure of clients, reducing the likelihood of a successful cyber attack.

We partner with some of the most innovative cyber security solution providers to counter the cyber threat. If a successful attack occurs, we have the experience and technical skills to minimise the potential damage.
