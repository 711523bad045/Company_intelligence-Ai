CAS Services

CAS provides comprehensive and secure document digitisation, information storage and facilities management services. For more than 25 years we have worked with NHS Trusts, financial services providers, and corporate and private clients. Our head office is just four miles from the City of London, supported by our advanced storage centres across the UK. Find out more about our services below:

Medical Records Storage

CAS are experts in patient and medical records storage, Scanning and management, with the approval of the NHS and FCA. Storing medical records securely off-site in CAS warehouses is not only cost-effective, but thanks to our RFID and live-file storage systems, it is highly efficient. We have an excellent track record of working with organisations whose document archive arrangements are complex and demanding.
